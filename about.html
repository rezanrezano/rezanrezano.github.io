A glimpse of my life:

Before Re:Coded

I was born in Syria.
I was studing in Syria, until I reached my first semester in university.
I got my baccalaureate in 2015 and then I moved to the university to study at the Faculty of Economics at Al-Furat University in Al-Hasakah,

Why I joined Re:Coded:

After leaving Syria and arriving in Iraq, I moved to live with my brother in Erbil in a Syrian refugee camp. I was planning to complete my university studies at a university in Erbil, but I was unable to present my school documents because I arrived at the end of 2015. At that time, the university registration deadline was over and I was thinking that I had lost one year of my studies. I decided to find something that would benefit me until the new school year. I'm good at computer maintenance, and I worked with my friend in computer and mobile maintenance. During a short period of work in that area, my brother told me about Re:coded, and its objectives.
I dreamed that one day I could become a developer of the video games and I saw that Re:Coded can help me achieve my dream.
I joined Re:Coded to become a skillful programmer,and reach my dream of becoming a game developer.
Now I managed to learn many coding Languages from Re:Coded, such as:
Ruby/Rails, AngularJS, JavaScript, jQuery, HTML5, CSS,SQL, VBA Access

Every thing in my life has improved with Re:Coded.
